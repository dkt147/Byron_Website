    Byron Case Is Innocent In Prison
    Wrongfully Convicted Of Murder

    For 20 years Byron has been a prisoner of the State of Missouri, his freedom ripped away for a crime he didn't commit.

      TWO LIFE SENTENCES,YET BYRON IS INNOCENT

      Byron was just a teenager in 1997, when his friend Anastasia WitbolsFeugen died of a gunshot to the head. Most people, including the officer running the investigation, believed Anastasia died in a suicide pact with her boyfriend, Justin Bruton. Hours after her death Justin shot himself dead. Her body hadn't even been identified yet.

      Three years passed. Then Byron broke ties with his ex-girlfriend, Kelly Moffett. Within days Kelly was telling Jackson County prosecutors that Byron had shot Anastasia. Two Jackson County prosecutors, eager to close the case, put Byron on trial.

      They withheld evidence and let key witnesses lie on the stand. At the end of it all, the jury found him guilty of first-degree murder and armed criminal action, and the judge sentenced Byron to life without the possibility of parole.

      Tomorrow for us will be just another day; for Byron it'll be another day taken away.

    Stand up for justice

    Fighting to free an innocent person imprisoned after a wrongful conviction takes persistence, strength, and intense hope that justice can be found. Lots of public support helps, too.

    Here are just a few simple things you can do to help win back Byron's stolen freedom.

    • Tweet Missouri Governor Mike Parson (@GovParsonMO), asking him to pardon Byron. using #FreeByronCase
    • Sign our petition to the governor.
    • Contribute to the Free Byron Case legal fund using Venmo.
    • Share the PDF slideshow on the case on social media.
    • Print a Free Byron Case flyer and hang it in a public space.
    • Buy a copy of Byron's book. When you finish reading it, pass it along to a friend.
    • E-mail us to request a FREE BYRON CASE T-shirt. Then wear it and be an ambassador for the campaign.
    • Post a selfie on Instagram, holding up a #FreeByronCase sign.
    • Share a link to this site with your Facebook friends.
    • Make a TikTok or YouTube video about the injustice of Byron's case.

    Write your own blog post, article, or essay on why Byron's story matters to you.
    Reach out to a blogger, journalist, producer, or director, and encourage them to look into this bizarre case of murder, betrayal, and wrongful conviction.
